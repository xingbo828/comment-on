So what exactly do we do? Here’s a brief overview of the features we offer:

  • An easy-to-use service inquiry form, optimized for all devices. We collect the customer information you need to better understand the size and scope of a service request. This makes service cost quotes more seamless and efficient for both you and your customers. Access the form from your profile, or link directly from your existing website.
  • A management dashboard. We provide a centralized dashboard so you can efficiently manage, sort and respond to incoming service requests.

Who our platform might be a good fit for:

  • You have a dated or non-existent online lead generation tool.
  • You wish to collect more specific and detailed information from prospective clients such as distance travelled, scale and address accessibility.
  • You would like to more easily automate and manage service inquiries and cost estimations.
  • You need a place to send customers to learn more about your services.
